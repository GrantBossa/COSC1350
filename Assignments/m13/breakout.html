Breakout Game

Level 1 : Your Score : 0

Instructions :

You earn 10 points per brick removed.

Use the left and right arrow keys to move the paddle.

Optionally, you can use the mouse over the screen to move left and right.

Hints:Remember to keep it on the screen. Move mouse slowly to maximize function processing.

The game is over when either all the bricks are removed or you miss the ball with the paddle.

Press the reset button to restart a game.
